FAQ's
Questions we can answer for you

How is the shipping process?

We will proceed to ship the product by courier, after 3-5 days you will receive the product. If you buy directly, you will be supported with 30% shipping fee.

Can I return the product if the product is defective?

We will solve problems related to product return policy if the product has problems or errors during delivery, to avoid counterfeiting, please record a video when unboxing the product. Please rest assured!

Does the store support shipping?

The shop will support the shipping fee depending on the existing promotions, if you order in the promotion, the store will support the shipping cost.

How to apply discount code?

In the shopping cart section, there will be a section to apply the promo code, please follow the instructions.

How can I see product reviews?

In the product details section, we have displayed a list of product reviews, you scroll down to the bottom of the page to see the list of reviews.

I have questions about the product preservation policy?

Product protection policy will be guaranteed by the store during packaging and shipping, if you have any problems, please contact the store as soon as possible.
